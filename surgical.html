About Me

I am passionate about the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Statistics, and Surgical Robotics. My focus is on exploring innovative applications using open-source data, particularly inspired by advancements like the da Vinci Surgical System. I aim to leverage data analysis, machine learning, and simulation to contribute to improvements in surgical ergonomics, image analysis, outcome prediction, and control systems within the medical robotics field. While proprietary data on specific systems like da Vinci is limited, I believe significant progress can be made by creatively utilizing publicly available datasets, research findings, and simulation tools.
